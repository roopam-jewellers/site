Our Story

For over three decades, Roopam Jewellery has been crafting exceptional pieces that celebrate life's most precious moments.

Founded in 1990 by master jeweler Marcus Sterling, our boutique began as a small workshop dedicated to preserving the traditional art of fine jewelry making. Today, we continue this legacy by combining time-honored techniques with contemporary design sensibilities.

Our mission is simple: to create jewelry that tells your unique story. Whether it's an engagement ring that symbolizes eternal love, a custom necklace that captures a special memory, or a family heirloom restoration that preserves history, every piece we create is infused with passion, precision, and purpose.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does custom jewelry design take?

Custom jewelry typically takes 4-6 weeks from initial consultation to completion. The timeline may vary depending on the complexity of the design and material availability. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options including 0% interest plans for qualified customers. We also accept all major credit cards and offer layaway programs for special occasions.

What is your warranty policy?

All our jewelry comes with a comprehensive lifetime warranty covering manufacturing defects. We also offer complimentary cleaning and inspection services for the life of your purchase.

Can you resize rings and modify existing jewelry?

Absolutely! Our expert jewelers can resize rings, modify settings, and update vintage pieces. We offer free sizing for the first year after purchase and competitive rates for all modification services.

Do you buy or trade existing jewelry?

Yes, we offer fair market value for gold, silver, and precious stones. We also provide trade-in credit toward new purchases. Bring your pieces in for a free evaluation.
