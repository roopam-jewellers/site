Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to both our website (roopam-jewellers.github.io) and our mobile application for gold and silver savings management.

1. Introduction

At Roopam Jewellery, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website and mobile application for managing gold and silver savings plans.

Important: By using our website or mobile app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and address for account creation and management
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID details as required for compliance with financial regulations
  • Contact Information: Details you provide when contacting us through forms or support channels
  • Payment Information: Banking details and payment method information for savings plan transactions
  • Biometric Data: Fingerprint or face recognition data (stored locally on your device) for app authentication

2.2 Usage Information

  • App Usage: Features used, time spent, navigation patterns within our mobile app
  • Gallery Interactions: Items liked, viewed, and interaction timestamps in our gold/silver gallery
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers for notifications
  • Location Data: Approximate location for service availability and fraud prevention (with your consent)

2.3 Technical Information

  • Website Analytics: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referral sources
  • App Performance: Crash reports, performance metrics, and error logs
  • Communication Data: Records of communications between you and our support team

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Purposes

  • Account Management: Create and maintain your savings account and profile
  • Service Delivery: Process gold and silver savings plans and transactions
  • Security: Verify your identity and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
  • Communication: Send account updates, notifications, and respond to inquiries
  • Gallery Features: Personalize your gallery experience and track your preferences

3.2 Secondary Purposes

  • Product Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services
  • Customer Support: Provide technical assistance and resolve issues
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements for financial services
  • Marketing: Send promotional content about our services (with your consent)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Firebase Security: We use Google Firebase with enterprise-grade security for data storage
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your personal information
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication and biometric security options
  • Regular Audits: Ongoing security assessments and vulnerability testing

4.2 Data Storage

  • Location: Data is stored on secure servers provided by Google Firebase
  • Retention: Personal data is retained for as long as your account is active, plus 7 years for financial records as required by law
  • Backup: Regular encrypted backups ensure data availability and integrity

5. Information Sharing

5.1 We Do NOT Share Your Information With:

  • Third-party marketers or advertisers
  • Social media platforms (unless you explicitly connect accounts)
  • Data brokers or analytics companies for commercial purposes
  • Any party for profit or commercial gain

5.2 We MAY Share Information Only When:

  • Legal Requirement: Required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help operate our services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Security: To prevent fraud, security threats, or illegal activities
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets (with user notification)
  • Consent: When you explicitly authorize information sharing

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Your Privacy Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal information we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Restriction: Request limitation of how we process your data

6.2 Communication Preferences

  • Notifications: Control which app notifications you receive
  • Marketing: Opt out of promotional emails at any time
  • SMS: Unsubscribe from text message communications

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 Website Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

7.2 Mobile App Tracking

  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance
  • Crash Reporting: Technical data to identify and fix bugs
  • Performance Monitoring: App speed and reliability metrics

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during such transfers, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by regulatory authorities
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant data protection authorities
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct

10. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that may affect your personal information, we will:

  • Notify relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by law
  • Inform affected users within 7 days of discovering the breach
  • Provide details about what happened and steps being taken
  • Offer guidance on protective measures you can take

11. Third-Party Services

Our app and website integrate with third-party services:

  • Firebase: For authentication, database, and hosting (Google Privacy Policy applies)
  • Payment Processors: For handling transactions (their privacy policies apply)
  • Analytics Services: For website and app performance monitoring

We carefully vet all third-party services and ensure they meet our privacy and security standards.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For significant changes, we will notify users via email or app notification
  • Continued use of our services after changes indicates acceptance
  • Previous versions will be archived and available upon request
